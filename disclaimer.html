The CashCalc denomination calculator is a convenience tool. While the arithmetic is straightforward (multiplication and addition), please note:

  • The calculator depends on the accuracy of the numbers you enter. If you miscount notes, the output will be incorrect.
  • The “amount in words” feature follows standard Indian English conventions but may not match the exact phrasing preferred by your specific bank branch.
  • Printed denomination sheets are convenience documents generated by your browser. They are not official bank forms and acceptance depends on the individual bank or branch.
  • The tool excludes the ₹2000 denomination because it was withdrawn from active circulation on 19 May 2023. If you need to count ₹2000 notes for exchange purposes, you will need to add that denomination separately.
